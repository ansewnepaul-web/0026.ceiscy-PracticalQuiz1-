UNDERSTANDING OUR LIVING PLANET

FORESTS

Forests cover nearly one third of the Earth's land surface and play a vital role in maintaining life. They regulate climate, store carbon, protect water sources, and provide habitat for millions of species.

OCEANS

Oceans cover more than seventy percent of the planet and influence global weather systems, absorb heat, and support complex marine ecosystems.

WILDLIFE

Wildlife includes all undomesticated living organisms that contribute to ecosystem balance and biodiversity.
